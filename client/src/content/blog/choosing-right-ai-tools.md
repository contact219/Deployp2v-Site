# Choosing the Right AI Tools for Your Business Size

The AI landscape can be overwhelming. With thousands of tools available, how do you choose the right ones for your small business?

## Start With Your Pain Points

Before looking at any tool, ask yourself:
- What tasks take up most of my time?
- Where do errors happen most frequently?
- What would I do with an extra 10 hours per week?

## Match Tools to Your Stage

### Solo Entrepreneurs
- Focus on personal productivity tools
- AI writing assistants for content
- Simple chatbots for basic customer queries

### Small Teams (2-10 people)
- Collaborative AI tools
- Automated workflow systems
- Customer service automation

### Growing Businesses (10-50 people)
- Custom AI solutions
- Integrated analytics platforms
- Advanced CRM with AI features

## Budget Considerations

Many powerful AI tools offer:
- Free tiers for testing
- Pay-as-you-go pricing
- Small business discounts
